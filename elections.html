Why should you vote?

🏘️
Local races matter most
School boards, county clerks, and city councils make decisions that affect your daily life more directly than federal offices.
📊
Your vote is statistically significant
Local elections are often decided by hundreds — sometimes dozens — of votes. Your vote carries real weight.
📋
Ballot measures change policy directly
Tax levies, bonds, and amendments are decided directly by voters — no politicians in the middle.
🗓️
Elections happen every year
Presidential elections get the attention, but primaries, special elections, and off-cycle races happen constantly.
